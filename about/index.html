# About me

I’m Bart, a web developer based in Nijmegen, the Netherlands. I’m currently employed as Development Team Lead at Linku, a digital agency where I work with a team of talented people to create bespoke web applications, websites and mobile apps.

Most of my development work is done with either PHP and Symfony or TypeScript and Angular, but I like to dabble in other languages and frameworks as well.

In my spare time I’m an avid musician (electric guitar and the tuba) and I like to tell people I’m a runner but in all honesty my Netflix account has way more activity in it than my Runkeeper’s.
